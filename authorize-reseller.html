Authorized Reseller

Eligibility Requirements

To be considered as an authorized reseller of Amaeyya and Prohall products, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Business Registration:Must be a legally registered entity in Mauritius with a valid Business Registration Number (BRN).
  • Experience:Proven experience in the retail, distribution, or e-commerce of beauty, cosmetic, or haircare products.
  • Infrastructure:Adequate facilities to store and display products under hygienic conditions, ensuring product safety and quality.
  • Financial Stability:Demonstrated financial stability to support inventory procurement and marketing efforts.
  • Brand Integrity:Strong commitment to maintaining and promoting the values and reputation of Amaeyya and Prohall brands.

Legal Clauses for Reseller Agreements

  • Exclusivity Clause:Resellers are prohibited from distributing counterfeit, unapproved, or competing products under the Amaeyya and Prohall brand names.
  • Pricing Policy:Resellers must adhere to J & J Healthcare's recommended retail pricing guidelines to maintain market consistency. Unauthorized price discounts or markups are not permitted without prior approval.
  • Marketing and Advertising Approval:All marketing materials, campaigns, and promotions involving Amaeyya and Prohall products require prior approval from J & J Healthcare to ensure brand compliance.
  • Product Handling and Storage:Resellers must ensure proper storage conditions to maintain product integrity. Any damage due to improper handling will be the responsibility of the reseller.
  • Compliance with Mauritian Laws:Resellers must comply with all applicable health, safety, and cosmetic regulations in Mauritius.
  • Non-Transferable Agreement:The reseller authorization is non-transferable and cannot be assigned to any third party without written consent from J & J Healthcare.
  • Termination Clause:J & J Healthcare reserves the right to terminate the agreement immediately in cases of non-compliance, brand misrepresentation, or damage to brand reputation.

Demographic Necessities

  • Retail and E-Commerce Presence:Resellers must have a strategic focus on both physical retail outlets and online sales channels to maximize product reach across Mauritius.
  • Geographic Coverage:Resellers should aim to serve key urban and suburban areas, ensuring broad accessibility for consumers island-wide.
  • Target Audience:Focus on diverse customer demographics, including beauty enthusiasts, health- conscious individuals, and professional haircare salons.
  • Customer Education:Resellers are encouraged to provide product information and guidance to customers to enhance their experience and satisfaction with Amaeyya and Prohall products.
